What this is about

What we actually mean by it.

Most companies already have what they need for AI: data, documents, well-rehearsed processes - and a good sense of where routine work eats time. AI integration doesn't mean reinventing everything. It means putting a language model or a smart search engine in the right places, finding, classifying or explaining in seconds what someone today browses for twenty minutes.

Specifically: we build document searches that look for meaning instead of keywords. We vectorise and index internal knowledge bases so employees can ask questions in plain language - with source references. We automate process steps that are manual today: classifying incoming requests, extracting from forms, validation, routing. And we build chatbots that go beyond FAQ.

Where this ends depends on how deep you want to go. A mini workflow that automates one specific task is in production within two weeks. A custom piece of software with AI thought through from the start takes longer - but it pays off where off-the-shelf tools aren't enough. More on that in the next section: Three depths of integration.

Depth of integration
From a small workflow to custom software.
AI integration isn't a binary yes/no. Three depths, depending on the goal and the maturity of your setup. We go as deep as you want - and we're honest when the smallest depth is enough.
Stage 1

Workflow automation

2–4 weeks pilot · small start

Extend existing tools with AI. No new software, no data modelling from scratch. Quick lever, measurable effect.

  • Email triage and classification of incoming requests
  • Translation pipeline for marketing or product copy
  • Simple chatbot on an existing website (FAQ, first contact)
  • Automatic summary of tickets or meeting notes
Concrete examples
Stage 2

Deep integration

2–3 months · embedded in your stack

AI seamlessly integrated into existing backends, databases and workflows. With data modelling, permissions, audit trail. For use cases that need more than a bot. Prerequisite: interfaces to your existing software (APIs, webhooks, DB access) are available or can be added.

  • Vectorisation & indexing of internal knowledge bases (RAG)
  • Semantic search in contracts, manuals, reports
  • CRM enrichment with pseudonymised AI profiles
  • Document pipelines: classification → extraction → validation → database
  • Connection to your existing software via REST/GraphQL/webhook
View our process
Stage 3

Custom software

several months · AI as core, not as add-on

Complete application built from the ground up, with AI as an integral part. Where off-the-shelf tools aren't enough and your processes are unique. This is where AI integration and software engineering merge into one delivery.

Stack & infrastructure
What we build with. And why.
A short answer to the two questions every data-protection officer asks: "which model?" and "where do the data sit?"

AI models - project-individual

Which model and which provider make sense depends on the use case and the data-protection requirements. We prefer Claude via AWS Bedrock EU, but we're explicitly not tied to one vendor.

Claude (Anthropic) via AWS Bedrock eu-central-1 - our preferred choice for most use cases
OpenAI GPT via Azure OpenAI Service in EU regions - when customers already work in the Microsoft ecosystem
Google Gemini via Vertex AI europe-west - alternative EU sourcing
Local & open-source models (Llama, Mistral) on your own infrastructure - when data must not leave your data centre

Infrastructure & data protection

Our own SaaS products run on an EU/CH infrastructure with maximum GDPR standards - that's the stack we build and run ourselves. For client projects the hosting is project-individual: on our stack, in your own cloud (Azure, AWS, Google), in an EU/CH data centre of your choice or on-premise. What counts are the standards.

Our stack: Hetzner Nuremberg/Helsinki for application and database servers
Our stack: Infomaniak Geneva as Swiss witness and VPN control plane
Our stack: IONOS Frankfurt for external, encrypted backups
Binding for all projects: tenant isolation, AES-256, TLS 1.3, audit logging - GDPR/FADP from day one
