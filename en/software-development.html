What this is about

Engineering without smoke and mirrors.

Software projects rarely fail because of the technology. They fail because of unclear requirements, badly chosen tech stacks, junior teams without experience, or architectures that fall apart at the first scaling event. With RC-Tech you get the opposite: direct line to technical leadership, a tech stack we use ourselves, and architecture decisions that come with reasoning.

We're specialised in SME SaaS and internal operations tools. Multi-tenant architectures, cleanly implemented APIs, PWAs for tablet-first workflows, data pipelines with audit logging. What many agencies sell off the shelf, we build with an actual understanding of what your business reality needs.

What sets us apart from 90% of Swiss providers: EU hosting and GDPR compliance aren't features, they're prerequisites. We host primarily on Hetzner Nuremberg/Helsinki and Infomaniak Geneva, complemented by other GDPR-compliant EU providers depending on project requirements - all with DPA standards and transparent sub-processor lists. Your customer data stays where it should.

Stack & hosting
What we build with. And why.

Tech stack

Tightly focused - not hype-driven. What we use is proven, maintainable and well supported by the community.

Backend: TypeScript · Node.js · Fastify/Express
Frontend: React · Vite · TanStack Query
Database: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
Hosting: Hetzner Cloud (Caddy reverse proxy), Docker-based
Auth: JWT, OAuth2, magic-link where appropriate
AI (optional): AWS Bedrock EU, Azure OpenAI EU, local LLMs

Hosting & compliance from day one

EU hosting isn't an add-on for us - it's standard. Data protection is architected in, not retrofitted.

Primary hosting: Hetzner Nuremberg/Helsinki (DE/FI)
Backup hosting: IONOS Frankfurt (DE), encrypted
Edge/WAF: Gcore Luxembourg (EU-only edges)
Witness/HA: Infomaniak Geneva (CH)
GDPR/FADP: DPA stack, TOM documentation, sub-processor register
Alternatives possible: your own cloud, on-premise, multi-cloud
