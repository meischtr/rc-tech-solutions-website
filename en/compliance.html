Use cases
What we actually do.
Six areas where Swiss SMEs face compliance pressure in 2026 - and where we can help with hands-on, lived experience.

FADP & GDPR audit

Inventory of every data flow, gap analysis, prioritised action plan. Switzerland-EU cross-border explicitly accounted for. Includes a documented report that holds up under an FDPIC or customer audit.

EU AI Act compliance

Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 is in force - and barely anyone has thought the stack through. We map your AI features against Annex III, clarify transparency obligations (Art. 50), assess third-country transfers, and deliver documented compliance.

DPA drafting

Data processing agreements per Art. 28 GDPR, tailored to your tech infrastructure. With sub-processor list, objection mechanism, clearly defined responsibilities. Lawyer-checkable, audit-ready.

TOM documentation

Technical and organisational measures, written down to match what's actually running. AES-256, RBAC, audit logging, backup strategy, tenant isolation. What's really in place, not what just sounds nice.

US-provider carve-out

Insurance pressure or Schrems II risk? We map your setup, identify US providers (including hidden sub-processors), evaluate migration paths, and plan the switch to EU contracting parties. We've done it ourselves - so we know what it costs.

Processing register & DPIA

Records of processing activities under Art. 30 GDPR - new build or modernisation. Where required: a Data Protection Impact Assessment per Art. 35 GDPR. Both done practically, without unnecessary bureaucracy, but audit-ready.
